Detailed Description

Sample the gamma distribution (with rate \( \lambda > 0 \) and shape \( k > 1 \)), the sum of \( k \) exponential distributions with rate \( \lambda \).

\( \text{PDF}(x) = \frac{\lambda^k}{\Gamma(k)}x^{k-1}\exp(-\lambda\,x) \)

This class uses the rejection sampling scheme proposed in

Cheng, R.C.H. "The generation of gamma variables with non-integral shape parameter," Journal of the Royal Statistical Socieity C, Vol. 26 (1977)

which samples a \( \lambda = 1 \) gamma distribution using a special log_logistic as a proposal distribution. The \( \lambda = 1 \) variates are internally scaled to any \( \lambda \) by redefining the timescale (if \( \lambda = 10 \), the \( \lambda = 1 \) variates are downscaled by 10).

Note
This class is named gammaDist (instead of simply gamma) to prevent confusion with the gamma function.
