Detailed Description

Sample the log-normal distribution (the log of the variates are normally distributed, with mean \( \mu \) and standard deviation \( \sigma > 0 \)).

\( \text{PDF}(x) = \frac{1}{x\,\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} \exp\left(-\frac{1}{2\,\sigma^2}(\log x - \mu)^2\right) \)

log_normal extends normal by redefining GetTwo() to return \( x = \exp (x_{\mathrm{normal}}^{}) \).
