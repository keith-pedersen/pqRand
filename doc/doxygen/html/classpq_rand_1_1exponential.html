Detailed Description

Samples from the exponential distribution (with rate parameter \( \lambda \)): \( \text{PDF} = \lambda \exp(-\lambda\,x) \).

This distribution has an invertible CDF: \( \text{CDF}^{-1} = - \frac{1}{\lambda}\log(1-u) \). This \( \mathrm{CDF}^{-1} \) becomes ill-conditioned in both tails, and therefore benefits from a quantile flip-flop.
